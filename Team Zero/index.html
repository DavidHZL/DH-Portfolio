Who Are We?

Team Zero is an aspiring Indie Game Development Team based in Lincoln, Nebraska whose goal is to create worlds players can get lost in. We are inspired to create the most enjoyable and detailed Indie Games possible and we will not hesitate to try new ideas never seen before in the gaming industry.

David Hanzl is the founder and director of the projects Team Zero undertakes. Inspired by video games of the past, David works to see this company succeed and bring unique experiences to players all over the world.
